The Apple iPhone 14 series was launched last month at the 'Far Out' event in Cupertino. There are 4 models in the series that include - iPhone 14, iPhone 14 Plus, iPhone 14 Pro, and iPhone 14 Pro Max. Except for the iPhone 14 Plus the other three models were available for purchase in September itself.

From October 07, 2022, Apple iPhone 14 Plus was also made available for purchase in India. Before you plan to buy the iPhone 14 Plus, you can go through the comparison between the prices and specifications of iPhone 14 Pro Max and iPhone 14 Plus models. Moreover, the dimensions, connectivity, and RAM of both models are the same. However, they differ in terms of rear cameras, color availability, battery life, and pricing.

    What is the screen size of iPhone 14 Plus and iPhone 14 Pro Max?
    Both iPhone 14 Pro Max and iPhone 14 Plus feature a 6.7 inches Super Retina XDR OLED display. However, the prices of both the models are different.


    What is the iPhone 14 Plus price in India?
    The iPhone 14 Plus price in India is Rs 89,900. While the iPhone 14 Pro Max is available at a starting price of Rs 1,39,900.


    Is the iPhone 14 Pro Max waterproof?
    The iPhone 14 Pro Max comes with IP68 rating. This means the smartphone is dust and water resistant for up to 30 minutes at a maximum depth of 19 feet.
